Asharfis Story


Asharfi Lal is almost 40 years old. He lives in one of India's poorest states, Uttar Pradesh. His area is a dry one, and if you go back just a few decades, you'll see that most of the people here had no access to clean water. But in 1981 the Indian government decided to change that, and quickly. In just ten years, thousands of wells were installed across Uttar Pradesh. That was good news for people in the '80s; but as it turns out, not so great for their kids. The UN's "Water Decade" (1981-1990) may have included a ton of new wells, but what it didn't include was a full-fledged maintenance program to keep them running over time... Read More
